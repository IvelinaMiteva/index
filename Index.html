FAQ

Everything you
need to know.

What does Invoice-to-Cash actually mean?
+
Invoice-to-Cash covers the entire process from the moment an invoice is issued to the moment payment is received and reconciled — including dunning, escalation, collections, and cash allocation. I2C automates every step using intelligent AI Agents.
Which EU markets does I2C support?
+
I2C is built for EU-wide operations. We support postal, e-invoice B2C and B2B (EDI), SMS, Email, Viber, and international paper letters — with local payment providers, multicurrency cross-border payments, and country-specific escalation flows across all EU markets.
How does I2C integrate with our existing systems?
+
I2C connects to your ERP, banking systems, and third-party tools via API imports and webhook management. Our Data Integration AI Agent handles the entire connection process — no manual setup required from your team.
How do the AI Agents work in collections?
+
Our AI Agents operate autonomously — matching payments, predicting late payers, sending communications, answering customer queries via chat or voice, and escalating cases based on predefined rules. They work 24/7 without human intervention.
Is I2C suitable for debt collection companies?
+
Yes. I2C is specifically designed for both client-facing billing and BPO-oriented debt collection companies. It supports multi-client management, payout settlement between clients and DC companies, bulk escalation editing, and full back-office portal access.
